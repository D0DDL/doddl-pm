Ready for Stage 2?

When your child starts grabbing the spoon and steering it, they're ready for Stage 2 — Building Control (12–18m).

Grip it, own it

The pincer grip arrives and suddenly your toddler wants to do everything themselves.

  • Pincer grip developing fast
  • Deliberately scooping food
  • Self-feeding for most of the meal
  • Frustrated if you try to help!
