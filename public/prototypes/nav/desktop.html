How the mobile drawer works
1
Hamburger icon top-left opens the drawer. Logo stays centred, basket and search on the right.
2
Drawer slides in from the left at 88% screen width. Tapping the overlay or ✕ closes it.
3
Each section expands inline on tap — one open at a time. No separate screen needed, keeps orientation clear.
4
Stage cards are a 2-column grid — tap any card to navigate to that stage page directly.
5
No hover preview on mobile — hover is a desktop-only behaviour. Mobile taps navigate straight to the page.
6
Bundle Box chip always visible at the bottom of the drawer — good for impulse and gift purchase regardless of where in the nav a parent is.
7
Account links at the very bottom — My account, Order tracking, Contact us. Low priority, stays out of the way.
